Information


Initially, this website was just a coding project. A way to use and present public data to people. But, very quickly, it became apparent that these weren't just numbers in a spreadsheet or statistics. These were people: men, women, and children, some innocent civilians, others "enemy combatants" in a fight against a country thousands of miles away.


As you browse this data, I'd like you to ask yourself one question:


Do these deaths make you feel safer?




Please visit the The Bureau of Investigative Journalism to learn more. Additionally, thank you to Dronestream, developed by Josh Begley for developing the database for this information.
